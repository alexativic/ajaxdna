Who We Are

AJAX DNA is at the forefront of the biotech revolution, transforming the way industrial and medical cells are developed and optimized. We provide companies using biological systems with an inexpensive and fast way to develop and deploy these systems. Our innovative approach utilizes a patent-pending technology to convert a base cell into any industrial or medical cell capable of producing a wide range of biomolecules.

Through our protein design consulting service and candidate cell predictor tool, we assist researchers and companies in identifying their optimal industrial or medical cells. At the same time, we are growing a huge competitive advantage in our core product, our cell builder technology. This patent pending technology allows us to build, test and optimize all candidate cells identified by our cell designer tool. The technology also allows to easily screen all the cells in a highly parallelized manner. This enables us to test all candidates simultaneously, identifying the best candidate cell immediately and in a single step.

Our goal is to simplify the creation of cells for industrial applications (such as producing new vegan proteins or repurposing waste) and medical applications (such as developing cells for cell therapies or producing conventional and novel drugs). We aim to make the process as straightforward as describing the molecules a cell needs to produce, where the cell should deliver these molecules, and in what concentration.

Our vision is that companies in the pharmaceutical, manufacturing and chemical, and food industries can reduce their costs by up to 80% in R&D and cell system development by using our technology. These cost savings could pave the way for a future where intensive R&D for previously economically unviable markets, such as treatments for rare diseases, becomes widespread. This future would see companies regularly employing bioproduction strategies, achieving a circular, bio-based economy.
