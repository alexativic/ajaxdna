Who We Are

AJAX DNA is at the forefront of the biotech revolution, revolutionizing the way industrial microorganisms are developed and optimized. Our innovative approach combines cutting-edge technology with deep expertise in genetics to empower industries with customized solutions.

From analyzing genetic networks to delivering tested and ready microorganisms, we are committed to transforming industrial production processes and enabling a sustainable future.
