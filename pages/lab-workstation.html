What we're validating now

Three tiers of proof. Zero black boxes.

We don't ask you to trust a model. We show you the evidence hierarchy that proves it — from pure kinetics to real-world chaos. Each tier validates a different layer of biological truth.

Tier 1 · Kinetic Engine

Controlled Steady-State Cultures

Chemostat cultures isolate individual biological actors under precisely controlled conditions. We extract validated kinetic constants — substrate affinity, growth rates, metabolic yields — with no environmental noise. This is the mathematical foundation of every prediction.

What we prove The causal laws of biology. If the constants are wrong, nothing downstream works.
Tier 2 · Interaction Bridge

Designed Microbial Communities

Synthetic communities in controlled environments where we know the inputs but let the biology self-organize. Competition, cross-feeding, biofilm dynamics — we validate that the Digital Twin understands emergent behavior, and perturbance based behaviours, not just isolated growth.

What we prove The interaction logic. Can the model predict what happens when species compete and cooperate?
Tier 3 · Stress Test

Real-World Environmental Data

We feed real perturbation events into the model — nutrient pulses, temperature shifts, seasonal transitions — and compare the predicted community response against observed reality. This validates the model's explanatory power under maximum uncertainty.

What we prove Resilience under chaos. If the model explains reality here, it's ready for any industrial condition.
